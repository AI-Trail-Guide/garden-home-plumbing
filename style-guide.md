Style Guide: Garden Home Plumbing & Drain LLC
Color Palette
Primary Green (brand color): #2D5F2D
Forest green. Used for the header background, section accents, and primary brand elements. Trustworthy, grounded, professional.
Primary Green Dark (hover states): #1E4A1E
Darker shade for button hover states and active links.
Accent Gold (call-to-action color): #C5932A
Muted brass/gold. Used for "Call Now" buttons, CTAs, trust badge highlights, and anything you want the visitor's eye to land on.
Accent Gold Dark (hover states): #A67A1E
Darker shade for CTA button hover states.
Background White: #FFFFFF
Main page background.
Background Light Gray: #F5F5F0
Alternating section backgrounds to create visual separation between sections. Slightly warm.
Text Dark: #2C2C2C
Primary body text color. Warm dark gray, not pure black. Easier on the eyes.
Text Medium: #5A5A5A
Secondary text, captions, supporting text below headlines.
Text on Dark Backgrounds: #FFFFFF
White text used on the green and dark gray backgrounds.
Footer Background: #1E1E1E
Very dark gray, nearly black. Used for the footer.

Typography
Heading Font: Merriweather (Google Fonts)
Serif font. Conveys professionalism and trustworthiness without being stuffy. Used for H1, H2, H3 headings.
Body Font: Source Sans 3 (Google Fonts)
Clean sans-serif. Highly readable on screens at all sizes. Used for body text, navigation links, buttons, and form fields.
Font Sizes (desktop):

H1: 42px, bold, line-height 1.2
H2: 32px, bold, line-height 1.3
H3: 24px, bold, line-height 1.3
Body: 18px, regular, line-height 1.6
Small/caption text: 14px, regular, line-height 1.5
Nav links: 16px, semi-bold
Phone number in nav: 18px, bold

Font Sizes (mobile):

H1: 30px
H2: 26px
H3: 20px
Body: 16px
Small/caption: 13px


Buttons
Primary CTA ("Call Now" button):

Background: Accent Gold (#C5932A)
Text: White (#FFFFFF), bold, uppercase
Padding: 14px 28px
Border radius: 6px
Hover: Background darkens to #A67A1E
Used for: Phone number CTAs, main action buttons

Secondary CTA ("See Our Services," "Read Reviews," etc.):

Background: Primary Green (#2D5F2D)
Text: White (#FFFFFF), bold
Padding: 12px 24px
Border radius: 6px
Hover: Background darkens to #1E4A1E
Used for: Internal navigation buttons, secondary actions

Outline Button (less prominent actions):

Background: Transparent
Border: 2px solid Primary Green (#2D5F2D)
Text: Primary Green (#2D5F2D), bold
Padding: 12px 24px
Border radius: 6px
Hover: Background fills to Primary Green, text turns white


Spacing and Layout
Max content width: 1200px, centered on the page.
Section vertical padding: 80px top and bottom on desktop, 48px on mobile.
Between elements inside a section: 24px.
Between heading and body text: 16px.
Card gap (in grids): 24px.

Specific Design Notes
Phone Number Treatment:
The phone number (503) 753-0225 should be impossible to miss on every page. In the nav bar, it appears as an Accent Gold button on the right side. On mobile, the phone button stays visible even when the hamburger menu is collapsed. In hero sections and bottom CTAs, the phone number should be large (at least 28px on desktop, 24px on mobile) and styled as a tappable button.
Trust Badge Placement:
On the homepage, trust badges appear in a horizontal row just below the hero. Each badge should have a simple icon or shield shape above the text label. Keep them understated, not flashy. The green and gold color palette should tie them together. On the About page, badges are listed with more detail in the credentials section.
Header/Navigation:
Sticky (stays at the top of the screen when scrolling). Background: Primary Green (#2D5F2D). Text/links: White. Active page link gets an underline or subtle highlight. The "Call Now" phone button in Accent Gold provides strong contrast against the green nav bar. On mobile, the hamburger menu icon is white. When opened, the mobile menu should slide down with a green background and white links.
Footer:
Background: Footer Dark (#1E1E1E). Text: White and light gray. Links turn Accent Gold on hover. Three columns on desktop, stacked on mobile. Generous padding so it doesn't feel cramped.
Image Placeholders (during build phase):
Use solid colored rectangles with the Background Light Gray (#F5F5F0) as the background and Text Medium (#5A5A5A) as the text color. Include descriptive text of what photo should go there. Maintain appropriate aspect ratios: hero images should be roughly 16:9, square images for team photos, and 4:3 for service images.
Cards (services, testimonials):
White background, subtle box shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)), border radius 8px, padding 24px inside. On hover, shadow increases slightly for a subtle lift effect.
Form Fields:
Border: 1px solid #CCCCCC. Border radius: 6px. Padding: 12px. On focus, border color changes to Primary Green. Label text in Text Dark, above the field.

Overall Vibe
This site should feel like a trustworthy neighbor who happens to be a great plumber. Not corporate. Not flashy. Not trying too hard. Clean, straightforward, professional, with just enough warmth to feel personal. The green says "established and reliable." The gold says "call us now." The white space says "we're not trying to overwhelm you." Every design choice should reinforce: "These are honest people who do good work."
