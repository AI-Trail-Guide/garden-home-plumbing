Meet Derek & Lani

The husband-and-wife team behind Garden Home Plumbing.

Our Story

Garden Home Plumbing & Drain started in 2012, but Derek's plumbing experience goes back more than 20 years. After serving in the military, Derek built his career in the trades, earning his plumbing license and learning the craft the old-fashioned way: one job at a time.

Lani is the reason our customers say the whole experience was easy. She handles scheduling, quotes, and follow-ups so nothing gets missed.

We're not trying to be the biggest plumbing company in Portland. We're trying to be the one you trust. We show up when we say we will, we're honest about what the problem is, and we charge a fair price to fix it. That's it.

About Derek

Derek is a U.S. military veteran and a licensed plumber with over 20 years of hands-on experience. He handles every job personally.

His customers consistently praise him for being straightforward, respectful of their homes, and thorough in his work. He doesn't cut corners and he doesn't upsell you on work you don't need.
